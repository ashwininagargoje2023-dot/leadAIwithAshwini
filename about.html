My story
Why I started
this

Most AI content is written by engineers for engineers. I looked around and realised the people who needed it most — the business leaders making $500K AI decisions — were being handed marketing decks and buzzwords instead of real depth.

I started leadAIwithAshwini to fix that. To be the one account where a VP, a CTO, a PM, or a founder can finally understand what is actually happening under the hood of the AI tools they are buying, building, and betting their organisations on.

I cover the full stack — from how a transformer processes a token to whether your organisation's AI maturity is ready for autonomous agents. No PhD required. No jargon. No hype. Just what you need to lead confidently in an AI-first world.

New content every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday on Instagram. Coaching, workshops, and advisory for when you need to go deeper.
